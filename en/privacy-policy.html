2. DATA WE PROCESS

When you use this website we may process the following categories of personal data.

Data you provide directly:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Team size
  • Project description
  • Any information included in your message

Technical and usage data

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Pages visited
  • Interaction events on the website
  • Language preference
  • Consent preferences
  • Limited analytics information where consent has been provided

3. PURPOSES OF PROCESSING

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries submitted through the contact form
  • To evaluate potential collaboration or project requests
  • To maintain communication with prospective clients
  • To improve the usability and performance of the website
  • To maintain website security and prevent misuse
  • To understand how visitors interact with the website where analytics consent has been given

4. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

Processing of personal data is based on the following legal grounds:

  • The data subject has requested information or contact regarding services, constituting pre-contractual steps.
  • Legitimate interest in managing professional enquiries and communications.
  • Consent, where required, particularly for analytics cookies and similar technologies.

5. CONTACT FORM PROCESSING

When you submit the contact form, the information you provide is transmitted to Formspree, which processes the submission and forwards it to our email inbox.

This information is used exclusively for evaluating and responding to enquiries submitted through the website.

Only authorized members of the PAMUUC ORGANIC CLOTHING S.L. team may access this information.

We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

6. DATA RETENTION

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

As a general guideline:

Contact enquiries may be retained for up to 24 months from the last meaningful interaction unless a business relationship begins or legal obligations require longer retention.

Analytics data retention follows the configuration of the analytics platform used.

Once the data is no longer necessary it will be deleted or anonymized.

7. RECIPIENTS AND SERVICE PROVIDERS

Personal data may be processed by service providers necessary for the functioning of the website, including hosting providers and technical service providers.

Google services such as Google Analytics and service providers such as Formspree may process limited technical and personal information required to operate the website or process enquiries.

8. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Some of the service providers used by the website may process data outside the European Economic Area. In such cases, appropriate safeguards under applicable data protection legislation are applied.

9. COOKIES AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES

This website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve website functionality, store language preferences, and measure website usage where analytics consent has been granted.

Further information is available in the Cookie Policy.

10. YOUR RIGHTS

Under applicable data protection law you may exercise the following rights:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability where applicable
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

How to exercise your rights

You may exercise your rights by contacting info@pamuuc.com.

If you believe your rights have been violated, you may lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Authority.

11. SECURITY

PAMUUC ORGANIC CLOTHING S.L. applies reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data processed through this website.

However, no internet transmission method or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

12. CHILDREN'S DATA

This website and its services are intended for professionals and organizations. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.
