Cookie Policy

Last updated: 9 March 2026

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES

Cookies are small text files or similar technologies that are stored on a user's device when visiting a website. They allow the website to remember certain information about the visit, such as language preferences or interaction choices.

2. TYPES OF COOKIES USED

This website uses the following categories of cookies or similar technologies.

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the website and do not require consent.

They may be used to:

  • remember language selection
  • store cookie consent preferences
  • maintain basic website functionality

Analytics cookies

With your consent, analytics cookies may be used to measure how visitors interact with the website.

These cookies help us understand:

  • page visits
  • navigation behaviour
  • interaction with content

Analytics provider

Analytics technologies on this website are implemented through Google Analytics.

3. COOKIE CONSENT

When you first visit the website, a cookie banner allows you to:

accept analytics cookies

reject analytics cookies

Analytics cookies are not activated until you provide consent.

Your choice is stored locally in your browser.

4. THIRD-PARTY COOKIES

If analytics cookies are accepted, Google Analytics may place cookies or similar technologies on your device to collect information about how the website is used.

5. MANAGING COOKIES

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • delete cookies
  • block cookies
  • control cookie behavior per website
  • clear stored website data

Effect of blocking cookies

Blocking certain cookies may affect some website features such as remembering language or consent preferences.

6. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect legal requirements, technological changes, or modifications to the website.
